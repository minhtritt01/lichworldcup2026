# Türkiye vs Paraguay — Group D Pre-Match Analysis

## Tactical Preview

Türkiye under Vincenzo Montella will look to use Hakan Çalhanoğlu as the deep-lying playmaker around whom their entire structure revolves. The Inter Milan midfielder's ball-carrying from deep and his range of passing provide the platform for Arda Güler to operate in higher zones with freedom, while Baris Alper Yılmaz and Irfan Can Kahveci offer directness on the wings. Paraguay, traditionally pragmatic under their CONMEBOL setup, will likely park a 4-4-2 defensive block, relying on Mathías Villasanti's physicality to disrupt Turkish build-up, and Miguel Almirón's ability to transition quickly from defense to attack to create dangers on the counter. The key tactical battle will be in central midfield: whether Çalhanoğlu can control space against the combined industry of Villasanti and Andrés Cubas. Cenk Tosun's physical presence up front provides Türkiye with a direct outlet if passing combinations break down, while Antonio Sanabria's aggressive pressing will test Türkiye's center-backs.

## Form & Momentum

Türkiye enter the tournament having impressed in recent UEFA competition, reaching their highest ranking in recent memory and developing genuine depth in midfield through the emergence of Arda Güler. The 20-year-old's debut season at Real Madrid exceeded expectations, and his World Cup debut carries enormous anticipation. The Turkish defensive organization, anchored by the experienced Merih Demiral and Samet Akaydın, is compact and physically strong. Paraguay represent a CONMEBOL qualifier that punches above the weight of individual talent through collective discipline. Miguel Almirón's experience with Premier League football at Newcastle adds an extra dimension of quality that is unusual for a side of Paraguay's resources. Their defensive block in CONMEBOL qualifying was difficult to break down, and several results against more technically gifted opponents demonstrated their capacity to frustrate and counter-punch.

## Predicted Lineup & Key Players

**Türkiye (4-3-3):** Mert Günok; Zeki Çelik, Merih Demiral, Samet Akaydın, Abdülkadir Ömür; Hakan Çalhanoğlu, Arda Güler, Salih Özcan; Baris Alper Yılmaz, Cenk Tosun, Irfan Can Kahveci

**Paraguay (4-3-3):** Antony Silva; Fabián Balbuena, Gustavo Velázquez, Omar Alderete, Junior Alonso; Mathías Villasanti, Miguel Almirón, Andrés Cubas; Ángel Romero, Óscar Romero, Antonio Sanabria

**Players to Watch:** Arda Güler is the player all eyes will be on — his ability to operate between the lines and produce moments of individual magic could unlock Paraguay's compact defensive structure. For Paraguay, Miguel Almirón's work rate in both phases is critical: his pressing disrupts Türkiye's midfield rhythm while his runs from deep provide the primary counter-attacking outlet.

## Score Prediction & Match Odds

Both sides carry genuine uncertainty, making this one of the more evenly matched Group D encounters. Türkiye's technical advantage in midfield is significant, but Paraguay's defensive discipline and physical presence make them difficult to break down over 90 minutes. Expect a tight, competitive encounter decided by a single moment of quality. **Prediction: Türkiye 1-0 Paraguay.** Arda Güler's creativity eventually opens Paraguay's structure after patient Turkish build-up, with the winner arriving in the second half. **Match Odds:** Türkiye 1.85, Draw 3.20, Paraguay 4.40.
