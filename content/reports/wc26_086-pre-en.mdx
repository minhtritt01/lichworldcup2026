# Argentina vs Cape Verde — Round of 16 Preview

## Tactical Analysis

Argentina are expected to deploy their familiar 4-3-3 formation, emphasizing possession and controlled build-up from the back. They'll likely dominate midfield tempo, with width provided through the wings. Cape Verde typically operates in a 4-4-2 or 5-4-1 setup, prioritizing defensive solidity and compactness. Argentina's key battle zone will be the midfield, where they must control the tempo before Cape Verde can organize their press. Cape Verde's defensive weakness lies in their wing coverage—if Argentina can establish dominance on the flanks, they can stretch the defense and create crossing opportunities. Argentina's potential vulnerability is a direct, aggressive counter-press from Cape Verde; maintaining possession and avoiding turnovers in dangerous areas will be critical.
