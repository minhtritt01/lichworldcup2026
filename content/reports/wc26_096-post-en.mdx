# Switzerland 0–0 Colombia: Switzerland Prevails on Penalties

Colombia grew into the match after the interval, forcing Kobel into two genuine saves from Rodríguez and Córdoba, but Switzerland's backline, marshaled by the commanding Akanji, refused to crack. The match became a battle of structures—Switzerland's control versus Colombia's conservation, neither yielding.
